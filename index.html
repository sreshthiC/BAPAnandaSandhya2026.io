About the Event

আনন্দ সন্ধ্যা

A Joyous Evening

✦

Ananda Sandhya is one of the most cherished annual events of the Bengali Association of Pittsburgh — a joyful evening woven together with music, dance, drama, and the warmth of community.

Join us for an unforgettable celebration of Bengali culture, featuring performances by talented community members spanning all ages. Come for the art, stay for the people.

A beloved tradition since 1981, Ananda Sandhya brings together the Pittsburgh Bengali diaspora every year for an evening of laughter, music, and belonging.

The Bioscope & Its Influencers

✦

This Year's Theme

Bengali culture has given the world some of its most influential voices in cinema. This year, Ananda Sandhya celebrates that legacy — the magic of the bioscope that once mesmerised village fairs, and the masters who carried that wonder onto the world stage.

Uttam Kumar
1926 – 1980

The undisputed Mahanayak — great hero — of Bengali cinema. His magnetic screen presence and nuanced portrayals defined an entire era of Bengali film, earning him an immortal place in the hearts of Bengal.

Satyajit Ray
1921 – 1992

A giant of world cinema. From the Apu Trilogy to Charulata, Ray's humane vision and painterly eye brought Bengali stories to global audiences, culminating in a Lifetime Achievement Academy Award.

Rituparno Ghosh
1963 – 2013

A poet of the silver screen who explored identity, longing, and the feminine with extraordinary sensitivity. His films — Unishe April, Dahan, Chokher Bali — reshaped modern Bengali cinema with literary grace.
